<h4>Notes<h4>
There are several versions of this work, known as the 70 chapter, 100 chapter,
and 120 chapter versions. The version provided here is the 120 chapter version,
which is also the version translated by John and Alex Dent-Young as 
<i>The Marshes of Mount Liang</i>.
