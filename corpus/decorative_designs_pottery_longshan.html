Longshan Culture 龙山文化

The Taosi 陶寺 site is located in Shanxi along the middle reaches of the Yellow River and is a part of the Longshan Culture spanning from circa 2,500 to 1,900 BCE. The picture of the plate below from the Taosi site has an early painted dragon.
