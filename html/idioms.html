Type a part of the idiom and click Find.

Enter all or part of the idiom you are searching for in either simplified or traditional Chinese. The results will be restricted to Chinese idioms. A Chinese idiom or 成语 is a four character set phrase commonly used in Chinese literature as a compact and erudite way of expressing an idea (Sun, Chaofen 2006. Chinese: A Linguistic Introduction, Kindle ed., Cambridge, UK; New York: Cambridge University Press, loc. 1282). Use the main page to search for other Chinese terms in addition to idioms.
